Career Services

As an SMU graduate, you have access to various services provided by the SMU Career Center to boost your career progress, expand your professional network, and sharpen your skills.

EVENTS & ANNOUNCEMENTS

Alumni are welcome to attend the annual SMU Career Fair that takes place on campus. This is a great opportunity for our current and former students, to learn about job and internship opportunities offered by our large network of corporate partners. As an alumnus, you are also invited to attend interesting presentations delivered by experts and professionals in the business world throughout the event. These presentations enable you to learn about the latest market trends and the most recent evolution and changes in both the local and international markets.

MEMBERSHIP CARD

The SMU Career Center is currently working on a membership card program for our alumni. The card would give them access to numerous advantages, such as a privileged discount on our executive programs tuition fees, free access to the SMU Library, special rates for room rentals, etc. Further details to be announced soon.

Alumni Giving

The SMU Career Center believes in the importance of peer-to-peer knowledge sharing, therefore, alumni are encouraged to give back to their alma mater by helping their fellow students in their next step. As an alumnus/alumna you can provide career advice, share your expertise to inspire current and prospective students by showing how SMU has influenced who you are, coach our current students, give presentations and take an active part in our workshops/events, etc. If you have any proposition, please feel free to fill in the form below.
